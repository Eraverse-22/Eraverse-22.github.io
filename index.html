About

01

I'm a Mechatronics Engineering student based in Egypt, building at the edge of robotics, embedded systems, and AI. My work spans the full stack of physical intelligence — from mechanical design in SolidWorks to ROS2 simulation to real-time embedded firmware.

My focus is on intelligent robotic systems: machines that sense, reason, and act. Currently deep in ROS2 + Gazebo simulation, ESP32-based control systems, and integrating local LLMs into hardware workflows.

I build to learn, document to share, and design for scale. Every project here is a step toward becoming a systems-level AI-integrated robotics engineer.
